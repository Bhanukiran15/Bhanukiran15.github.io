About Us

Crafting Unforgettable Moments

Welcome to Sri Infinity Convention, the pinnacle of luxury and elegance in Hyderabad. Nestled in the heart of Manikonda, we are more than just a venue; we are the canvas for your most cherished memories. Our mission is to transform your events into timeless celebrations.

From grand weddings to corporate galas, our state-of-the-art facilities, meticulous attention to detail, and unparalleled service ensure every occasion is a seamless and spectacular success. At Sri Infinity, your vision is our command.

Our Exquisite Features

Main Hall

A majestic, pillar-less hall with soaring ceilings and customizable ambient lighting, designed to host the grandest of celebrations and corporate events.

Ample Parking

Extensive, secure parking space with valet services, ensuring convenience and a hassle-free arrival for all your distinguished guests.

Bar Room

A sophisticated, fully-stocked bar with contemporary decor. The perfect setting for your guests to mingle and enjoy exquisite cocktails.

Elegant Dining

A dedicated, opulent dining area that offers a world-class culinary experience, with customizable menus crafted by our expert chefs.

Banquet

Perfect for more intimate gatherings or pre-function events, our banquet spaces offer versatile arrangements and luxurious decor.
